What is Farm-To-Table

Farm-to-table is a movement that promotes serving locally grown food directly to consumers, led by restaurants, developers, or even grocery stores. The Farm-to-Table Movement, also called Farm-to-Plate began to emerge as consumer preferences evolved. Farm-to-Table is also good for the environment, as practicing farm-to-table habits can reduce the carbon footprint due to less transportation of goods.
